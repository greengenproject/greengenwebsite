About Us

Our story.

We are a youth organization founded by by Rephael Raagas and Gwyneth David, scholars of the Philippine Science High School - Central Luzon Campus, in association with the World Wide Fund for Nature - Singapore under the #WeGotThis Youth Sustainability Incubator Programme.

Our Mission

To provide SDG-based climate education to Filipino youth communities, empowering them to be champions of just transition.

Our Vision

We envision the youth of today transforming into a generation that actively confronts climate challenges, demands accountability from policymakers, and advocates for sustainable practices in all sectors.

Our Strategy

Synergize

because climate solutions cannot be done alone.

Discuss

because education is the precursor to positive action.

Grow

because learning must grow into real-life solutions in order to change the world.

Our Team

Meet the Team!

Our team consists of a diverse set of passionate youth dedicated to creating change within their communities. Our directors and volunteers are from all over the Philippines, working together to achieve our common mission.
